Task 3.2.1.c.
Choose one (or more) of the following vertex arrangements, \(3.6.6\text{,}\) \(4.6.6\text{,}\) \(3.4.4.4\text{,}\) \(3.4.3.4\text{,}\) \(3.10.10\text{,}\) or \(5.6.6\) and construct the semiregular polyhedron that has that vertex arrangement. If you are up for a challenge, you may choose \(3.4.5.4\text{,}\) \(3.3.3.3.4\text{,}\) or \(3.3.3.3.5\text{.}\)
  1. How many faces of each type does your semiregular polyhedron have?
  2. What is the total number of vertices?
  3. What is the total number of edges?
