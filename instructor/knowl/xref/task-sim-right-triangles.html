Task 5.3.1.a.
Figure 5.3.1 gives a triangle \(\Delta ABC\) and its image \(\Delta A'B'C'\text{.}\) Use it to complete the following.
  1. Determine the center \(O\) of the dilation.
  2. Determine \(\frac{OA'}{OA}\text{,}\) \(\frac{OB'}{OB}\text{,}\) and \(\frac{OC'}{OC}\text{.}\) What does this tell you about the dilation?
  3. Is it true that \(\frac{OA'}{AA'}=\frac{OB'}{BB'}=\frac{OC'}{CC'}\) ? Are these equal to the scale factor?
  4. Is it true that \(\frac{A'B'}{AB}=\frac{B'C'}{BC}=\frac{A'C'}{AC}\) ? Are these equal to the scale factor?
  5. Which pairs of angles must be congruent?
