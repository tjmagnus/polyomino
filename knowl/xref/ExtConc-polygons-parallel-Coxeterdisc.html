Exercise 5. The Hyperbolic Disk Model.
The illustration in Figure 2.3.13 was explored by mathematician H. S. M. Coxeter and used by artist M. C. Escher as a basis for his Circle Limit series of prints. This illustration suggests another type of non-Euclidean geometry, a disc model of hyperbolic geometry. Here, lines are of two types: diameters of the outer disc and circular arcs that meet the outer disc at a right angle
