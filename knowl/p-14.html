Geometry is all around us, in nature, in architecture, in art, in games, and in technology. Throughout history, humans have marveled upon geometrical patterns, investigated possibilities, developed geometrical tools, and shared these discoveries with others. Mathematicians use logic to confirm what they appear to see and to convince other scholars. The theorems and formulas that you have likely encountered in previous classes were developed and refined over the last two millenia.
