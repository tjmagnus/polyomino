Formula 3.4.10. Volume of a Cone.
The volume of a cone is one-third the volume of a cylinder with the same base and height. Since the base of a cone or cylinder is a circle with area, \(A_{base}=\pi r^2\text{,}\) the formula for the volume of a cone can be written as
\begin{equation*} V_{cone}=\frac{\pi r^2 h}{3} \end{equation*}
where \(r\) is the radius of the base and h is the vertical height when the cone is sitting on its base.
