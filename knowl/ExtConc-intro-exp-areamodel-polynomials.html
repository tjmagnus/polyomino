Exercise 11. Modeling the Multiplication of Polynomials.
The sketch below illustrates a product of polynomials.
  1. Write the height of the entire rectangle as an algebraic expression involving the variable \(x\text{.}\)
  2. Write the width of the entire rectangle as an algebraic expression involving the variable \(x\text{.}\)
  3. Algebraically compute the area of the entire rectangle as the product of your two algebraic expressions. Show your work.
  4. Find the area geometrically by finding the areas of the four subrectangles and adding the areas together. Show your work. Did you get the same result?
