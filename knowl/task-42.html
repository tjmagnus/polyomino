Task 2.2.2.e.
To measure the angles on a sphere, pass a piece of thread through the vertex hole of a protractor and tie it securely. Hold the protractor with that hole at the vertex of the angle you wish to measure, aligning the baseline with one side of the angle. Use the thread to straighten and lengthen the second side of the angle so that you can read the measure on the protractor. Measure the three angles of your triangle and then add the three measures.
