In the Geogebra applet, click and drag a vertex of \(\Delta ABC\) to change the shape of your triangle. The rotations should now be of your altered triangle. Do your observations in the previous questions still hold?
Geogebra makes exploring multiple possibilities easier. If you are doing this activity with paper triangles, experiment with other triangles, repeating the previous tasks. If your classmates used different triangles, this would be a good time to compare results.
