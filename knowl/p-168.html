(Skip if this doesn’t apply to your (de)construction.) Relate any new labels to the trapezoid’s height, base, and/or summit. For example, if you split the base into three segments, labeled \(x\text{,}\) \(y\text{,}\) and \(z\text{,}\) you would write down the relationship \(x+y+z=b_2\text{.}\) If you split the base into two segments, one of which is congruent to the summit, you would write \(x+b_1=b_2\) or equivalently, \(b_1=b_2-x\text{.}\)
