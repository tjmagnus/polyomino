Task 1.1.2.b.
Observe how a larger copy of the L-pentomino tile can be created by filling it with four different pentominoes:
  1. How are the height and width of the new larger L-tile related to the height and width of the basic L-tile?
  2. How is the area of the new larger L-tile related to the area of the basic L-tile?
  3. Choose another pentomino from among F, I, P, T, U, W, y, and Z, and demonstrate how it can be created using four distinct polyominoes. Note that this cannot be done with X or V.
