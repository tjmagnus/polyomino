    Chapter 3 Polyhedra

    In this chapter, we will explore three-dimensional solids. Solids with flat polygonal sides, like cubes and pyramids, are called polyhedra. Other solids, like cylinders and spheres have curved faces.
    To fully understand this chapter, you will need to construct some of these figures. Be prepared to print copies of patterns on paper or cardstock so that you can fold and tape the figures together as needed.
